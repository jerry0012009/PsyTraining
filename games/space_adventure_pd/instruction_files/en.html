To summarise:


Your task is to collect as much fuel as possible by travelling from planet to planet.

You can travel either one step in the clockwise direction ('M'-key) or jump to a more distant planet ('Y'-key) following the jump pattern.

Different planet types can either bring you fuel or make you lose fuel.

The bar at the top of the screen shows you how much fuel you currently have.

When you jump, it sometimes happens that you land on the left or right neighbouring planet

instead of the expected target planet. This happens particularly frequently in planetary systems with asteroids in the background,

but can also (rarely) happen in planetary systems without asteroids.

The different probabilities of how often you miss the target planet

remain the same within the respective condition (i.e. with and without asteroids) during the entire experiment.
